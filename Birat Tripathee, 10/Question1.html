a litte bit about Electronics

Basic Components: Electronics often involves the use of basic components like resistors, capacitors, inductors, and diodes, along with more complex devices such as transistors and integrated circuits (ICs). These components are used to control and manipulate electrical currents and voltages. Electronic circuits are arrangements of components and wires that perform a useful task.

Semiconductors, like silicon, are materials with electrical properties between conductors and insulators. Transistors, which are fundamental in electronics, are semiconductor devices that amplify and switch electronic signals. Digital Electronics: Digital electronics deal with discrete voltage levels.
